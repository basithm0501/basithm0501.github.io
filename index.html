These experiences not only allowed me to share my knowledge but also showcased my dedication to teaching and inspiring the next generation.

Throughout my journey at RampedResults, I have also had the opportunity to serve as a presenter, conducting demonstrations on Robots and Virtual Reality at various public and private schools.
